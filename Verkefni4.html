Project 4 and 5, my contribution.

I worked in this groupproject with my colleges Sindri Snær and Björgvin Þór.
My role in the group was to make a video of the manufacturing process. I had no prior experience editing or making videos before this assignment so the first thing I did was to search on google for some good editing software. I chose to use VideoPad for the editing, but I didn't realise until I was into making the video that I could only export the video file for a limited amount of times.
For that reason I decided to switch to the Windows video editing app, which is very easy to use. I only had to import all of my clips into the app, and than put them in a good timeline. The image below shows how the app looks when I was arranging the photos.

The next step was then to choose some background music for the video. I searched on Youtube for an approriate song until I found one. Than I had to convert the song from the youtube video to an mp3 file. I used this website to convert it. Than I imported the mp3 file to Windows video editor and put it as the background sound. The picture below shows how that is done.

Now the video was ready to be exported, and uploaded to the website.
